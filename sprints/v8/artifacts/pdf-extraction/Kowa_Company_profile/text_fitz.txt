Management strengths 
With 22 years of experience in the plastic industry and strong ties with major domestic 
and overseas manufacturers, leveraging credibility, connections, expertose and a 
wealth of trading experience, we have distribution warehouses in each region to 
provide stable purchasing routes and regularly supply good plastic raw materials to 
each company.  
We comprehensively analyze the characteristics, shape, deposits, additives, etc. of 
resins, and provide total solutions related to waste plastic recycling, from raw material 
procurement to proposals for recycled raw materials that meet the needs of your 
company."Whole service" from purchase to classification, crushing, pelletization, 
customs clearance, and export with our own distribution route and management 
system. We make all employees always have a spirit of challenge and have the idea of U
co-prosperity and coexistence with customers. 
We collect plastics domestically and export them overseas. The exported plastics are 
reprocessed into various products and shipped worldwide.We have established and are 
operating a crushing factory GP Polymer, and are also focusing on remanufacturing in 
Japan.Loss materials and waste plastics purchased from companies are crushed and 
incorporated into recycling to circulate valuable resources. We aim to make effective 
use of resources so that we can carefully utilize the limited global resources and pass on 
the blessed global circulation to the next generation.
